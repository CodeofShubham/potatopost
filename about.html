Our Ridiculous Origin Story

It all started with a simple, yet profound, question in the mind of our founder, Komal Singh: "What is the most delightfully absurd thing one could possibly receive in the mail?"

The answer arrived not in an epiphany, but in a bag of groceries: The humble potato.

In a world drowning in emails, notifications, and boring bills, Komal realized the potato held the key to true, tangible, and completely unexpected joy. It was the antithesis of predictable communication.

Potato Post was born from the mission to champion the weird and the wonderful.
